About us

Itech Company provides the network of the stores of Indoor and Outdoor power products under brands ‘Forest & Garden’ and ‘Home & Garden’ with a wide range of products for professional use. The sales activities are supported by 3 big service centers with a total area of more the 300 m2

Since 2014 the iTech Company takes part in the tender activities with State Tender Internet platform Prozorro. A lot of big companies became the customers of our Company from this period. Some of them are budget-generating in their industries.

The main goal of the Company is to meet the needs of the customers. That’s why most employees of the Company work here 4-5 and more years. The sales manager is not only the guy who will pack the product for you. He has to know the technological process as well as the product range of different manufacturers. He has to talk with a professional user with one language. Only this could be the basis of success.

All members of the iTech team take part in the different hands-on trainings organized by representatives of different brands. Besides that every year our company organizes its own big hand’s on training with the theoretical part and practice as well. All members of the training have the opportunity to test a lot of different products and to understand all details of this or that technological process. Thousands of regular customers are the best proof that we are moving in the right direction. And we will try to become better to provide fast and qualified service for our customers.
